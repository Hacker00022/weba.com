Finance Policy

Logo Zeal Finance Policies

Purpose

Purpose: The purpose of this finance policy is to ensure proper financial management and accountability within Logo Zeal, enabling the company to operate efficiently, maintain financial stability, and support its strategic goals.

Financial Planning and Budgeting

Annual Budget: A comprehensive budget shall be prepared annually, detailing expected revenues and expenditures. This budget must be approved by the management team before the start of the fiscal year.

Monitoring: The budget will be monitored on a monthly basis, and significant variances will be reviewed and addressed by management.

Revenue Recognition

Invoicing: Invoices shall be issued promptly upon the completion of services or delivery of goods. All invoices must include detailed descriptions of the services rendered or products delivered.

Payment Terms: Standard payment terms are net 30 days from the date of invoice, unless otherwise agreed upon in writing.

Expense Management

Authorization: All expenses must be pre-approved by authorized personnel. Receipts or documentation must be provided for reimbursement requests.

Expense Reporting: Employees must submit expense reports within 15 days of incurring the expense. Late submissions may result in non-reimbursement.

Client Payment Policy

Advance Payments: For all projects, a minimum of 50% of the total project cost must be received before work begins. The remaining balance is due upon project completion and before final delivery.

Milestone Payments: For larger projects, payments may be divided into milestones as agreed upon in the project contract. Each milestone payment must be received before work on the next phase commences.

Project Manager Responsibility

Client Non-Payment: If a client fails to pay for an ongoing project, the project manager will be responsible for covering the cost of manpower used on the project. This policy ensures accountability and encourages proactive client management.

Financial Reporting

Monthly Reports: Financial statements, including income statements, balance sheets, and cash flow statements, will be prepared monthly and reviewed by management.

Annual Audit: An independent audit will be conducted annually to ensure financial transparency and integrity.

Compliance and Ethical Standards

Regulatory Compliance: Logo Zeal will comply with all relevant financial regulations and tax obligations. Any non-compliance issues will be addressed promptly.

Ethical Conduct: All employees are expected to adhere to the highest standards of ethical conduct in financial dealings, avoiding conflicts of interest and ensuring transparency.

Review and Amendments

Policy Review: This finance policy will be reviewed annually by management and updated as necessary to reflect changes in business practices or regulatory requirements.

Amendments: Any amendments to this policy must be approved by the management team and communicated to all employees.
